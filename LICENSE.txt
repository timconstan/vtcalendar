VTCalendar

Product Description:
VTCalendar is a web-based event calendar that allows for decentralized data input and includes 
a central approval mechanism. It also supports calendar hosting for individual organizations 
and the forwarding of events from these individual calendars to the main event calendar.
http://vtcalendar.sourceforge.net/
